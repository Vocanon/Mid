Vocanon's race orginated on earth they were known as the Neths. That was until a incident 7.5 millium ago happened, the details which are writen down in ancient Netherian texts. They ended up in the galaxy of unity, on the planet that would be later know as Volcania. They were give new abilities and the leader at that time became the first guardian of Volcanic and Techtonic activity He along with a mysterious being know as Drakian, built the ancient forge, and created the weapons that all four guardians would use. Sadly the leader died in battle but before he died he predicted that one day his descendant would become a legendary being. There was great evil that would someday arise and threated all of not just the galaxy of unity but the entire universe. Over time the Netherians grew from a small race into a kingdom that turned in the Great Netherian empire, than into an empire that controlled the entire planet of Volcania. Eventually they made it to space and started colonizing other planets in the local arm of the galaxy. God was the center of the entire life of most of Volcania, and was the source of the power that the guardians would wield The Guardians of Volcanic actity thoughout the generations visited earth during important events in earth's history Even one who saw, Jesus's ministry, and crucified
