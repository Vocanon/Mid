  • Herobrine and Lunia:Vocanon's father and mother.

  • Vocanon and Flama: The main character and his wife.

  • Radiom and Xenon: Vocanon's brother and nephew . "note in my story I still have not changed their names from boulder and rocky" Radiom is the guardian of Electromagnetic radiation, and elements until he dies and than Xenon takes over as guardian.

  • Flamon, Pyro/Pyrothian, Blaze, Flare, Basalan, Fissurian: Vocanon's sons

  • Diamondica, Emeraldia, Ruby, Sapphireia, Crystallina, Jadia: Vocanon's daughters

  • Thomas, and Magmortus: Vocanon's uncles. Magmortus gave on his right to the throne of Netheria in order to watch over the ancient forge.

  • Lavador:Vocanon's dead grandfather, and previous Guardian of Volcanic and techtonic activity

  • Drakian Draconicus: mysterious and powerful ancient being, the first guardian, and the Guardian of Dragons and oceans. Drakian is also Vocanon's mentor, and co founder of the Nether Empire, and ally.

  • Mr Terra and Terranius: Mr Terra was the guardian of Terrestial Life until he died. Vocanon found and trained his replacement Terranius. Both Mr Terra and Terranius live on Earth in the Milkyway galaxy.

  • Reglith Lunaquius: one of the few remaining members of a race that Lunia is connected to

  • Moltenia: Vocanon's twin sister who ran away during the terrible event that happened when Vocanon was around 10 or 12 years old

  • Volcan: the ancient founder of what would later become the Nether Empire and the first guardian of Volcanic and Tectonic activity

  • Necromia: Necromia is a great evil being who has killed all life in countless galaxies and creates undead armies. He can not die, he is the son of the devil and a witch. He has done unspeakible evil and attrocius actions. He is the antichrist in the story

  • Necrom:Necromia's evil ancestor who was killed by Volcan, but gave Volcan the wounds that would shortly after killed Volcan

  • The Ethnic Netherians: The Netherians traditionally refeared to the people of Volcania. They are a very technologically advanced race. They have technology 2 millium ahead of humanity.

  • Pyrocian Grounder, Vocanon's descendant who writes down the story


  • The Ancient Forge: the ancient forge is a huge 7.5millium year old super facility. Here the swords of each of the guardians where created. It has many big chambers and wings for all 4 guardians, it is three stories, and was built into a habitable moon.

  • Volcania: The capital planet of the entire Nether Empire

  • The Tomb of Volcan: an very ancient structure where Volcan was buried, and where the sword of Volcan found Vocanon

  • The Netherian Castle: The seat of the Netherian Empire, it is as tall as the rocky mountains. It is extremely old,and big, the oldest parts are over 6.5 millium old yet, the castle only became stronger. The castle itself is practically its own city.

  • Oceania and its moons: Oceania is home to Drakian and the Dracoians, it is mostly a ocean planet. It part is an autonomous solar system of the Nether Empire

  • The ancient seal: the acient seal is a seal that was put on earth to keep four great evil demonic creatures locked away. these creatures called destroyers are unkillable creatures, that are pure evil the are the opposite of the guardians, and where sealed away by the guardians 7 millium ago.

  • The dimensional hallway: it is a pocket dimension that can take a guardian to any part of the universe they need to be.

  • Nether Empire: The normally peaceful but powerful empire that rules over the entire Galaxy of Unity Used to be just in an arm of the galaxy of unity until the Vocanon era;
